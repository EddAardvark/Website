Mandelbrot Movie 3

This movie zooms in on the point (0, 0, 0.27322626, 0.595153338), each frame being roughly 5% deeper than the previous one. At 24 frames per second that's about a factor of 3 each second. The final frame is about one trillion times the resolution of the first.
