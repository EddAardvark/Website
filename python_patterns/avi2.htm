Mandelbrot Movie

(0,0,-0.0033,0.6380) − (0.3,0,-0.0033,0.6380)

This movie is quite spectacular, it shows waves of spiral patterns washing over one of the tangental circles. It starts off at (0,0,-0.0033,0.6380) and moves in increments of (0.0005,0,0,0) to (0.2995,0,-0.0033,0.6380). That's 600 frames (24 seconds @ 25 frames per second). It starts off a bit slow buts gets quite exciting towards the end. The views are in the Mandelbrot plane.
