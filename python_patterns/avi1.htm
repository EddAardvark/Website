Mandelbrot Movie

(0.0, 0.0, 0.32, 0.545) − (0.0, 0.985, 0.32, 0.545)

This movie contains nearly 1000 frames and shows scenes along the line (0.0, 0.0, 0.32, 0.545) to (0.0, 0.985, 0.32, 0.545), the increment being a constant (0, 0.001, 0, 0). The original images were rendered at 400 x 300 with a magnification of 20. The images are slices parallel to the Mandelbrot plane (-tM option) so the first images is actually on the Mandelbrot plane. The subsequent images are offset from this plane by increasing distances in the Zy direction.
