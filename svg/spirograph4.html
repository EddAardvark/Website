Animated Spirograph

This page draws animated spirograph patterns. Click on any of the "Randomise" buttons to randomly change the pattern. The "Stop" and "Start" buttons do what they say. You can also change the number of points used to draw the pattern, again randomly.

This is a simplified version of the spirograph drawer which gives you full control over all the parameters.
